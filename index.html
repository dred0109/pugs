Why pugs?

Adorable

This breed is the ideal house dog. Pugs are very happy living in the city or country. They need to move a lot.

Sweet

Pugs are playful dogs, ready for games any time,they are also lovers, and must be close to their human.

Loving

They are very intelligent, but they can be willful and stubborn, which makes the whole training challenging.

Loyal

Small and quiet breed, inactive when indoors, they are a good choice for apartment dwellers.

Charming

Pugs are greedy eaters and will overeat if given the chance. Since they gain weight easily.

Healthy

Pugs are generally healthy, but like all breeds, they're prone to certain health conditions. .
